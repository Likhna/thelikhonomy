Credit System Model:
The economy is a cross of a market economy system and a barter economy system. The fundamental principles of this economy are in accordance to the Japanese system of rapid economic growth.
Market is generated by the activity of the users on the platform. Inflation will be prevented by an ease on the credit and the price of purchasing the same.
Actions such as pushing a document to the global community, having the same promoted or collaborated on (which in turn generates a Erdos Number sort of thing) effects the credit. 
Credits are the means by which Papercasters make their revenue. User pay a fee in credits to Papercasters in order to have access to what they push into their public feeds and more refined works.
